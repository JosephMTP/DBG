# üçé Fruit Box Game

A number matching puzzle game where you drag to select apples and remove them when their sum equals 10!

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. You'll see a 17√ó10 grid filled with numbers from 1 to 9
3. Click and drag to select a box of apples (cells)
4. The selected sum is displayed at the top
5. When the sum equals **10**, release the mouse button
6. The selected apples will be removed and replaced with new random numbers!
7. Try to clear as many apples as possible and achieve a high score!

## Game Features

- **17√ó10 Grid**: A large playing field with 170 cells
- **Random Numbers**: Each cell contains a random number from 1 to 9
- **Drag Selection**: Click and drag to select a rectangular area of cells
- **Sum Matching**: Remove cells when their total equals exactly 10
- **Visual Feedback**: Selected cells are highlighted in blue
- **Score System**: Earn points for each successful removal

## Game Mechanics

- Click and hold on any cell to start selecting
- Drag to create a rectangular selection box
- The sum of all selected cells is shown in real-time
- When the sum equals 10, release to remove those cells
- Removed cells are automatically refilled with new random numbers
- Keep playing to achieve higher scores!

## Tips

- Look for combinations that add up to 10 (e.g., 1+9, 2+8, 3+7, 4+6, 5+5, or multiple numbers like 1+2+3+4)
- You can select any rectangular area, not just single cells
- Plan your selections to maximize your score!
